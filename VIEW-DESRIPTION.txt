Description of Each View:

• Admin View:
	Add, modify and remove doctors and receptionists and  the ability to add and modify patients records, but cannot remove it due to HIPPA Regulations.
	Modify the inventory and add the equipments.

• Doctor View:
	Add and remove a receptionist.
	Create, modify and remove treatment plans of patients.
	Add and remove prescripiton of patients.

• Receptionist View:
	Add and modify patients.
	Control the billing of patients.
	Modify the inventory in case the clinic runs out of prescriptions or medication.

• Patient View:
	Show the dental record of the patient in detail.
	Show the past and future appointments.
	Include the radiology, prescriptions, treatment plans and referrals.
